Recreation in Startopia

Recreation

In Startopia, people prioritize socializing as their primary form of recreation. Engaging with others and building connections is a popular and fulfilling way to spend leisure time.

The following aspects contribute to a healthy and enjoyable recreational environment:

  • No phones or social media
  • No overindulgence in food for pleasure
  • Crossbreeding of fruits for enhanced taste and nutritional value
  • Genetic modification of plants to convert sugar to sugar alcohols, reducing negative effects while maintaining the same taste
  • Outsourcing sugar to Erythritol, a sugar substitute that has no adverse health effects but delivers the same taste
  • A wide range of sports available with advanced technology ensuring maximum protection and preventing injuries
  • Board games like chess are popular among kids, though, Monopoly is banned due to its lack of enjoyment

Healthcare and Drugs

Startopia places great importance on health and well-being. The healthcare system prioritizes tailored medications and the absence of harmful drugs. Key features include:

  • Only drugs specifically tailored for healthcare purposes are available
  • No harmful drugs are permitted
