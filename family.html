Family in Startopia

Family Structure

Families in Startopia typically consist of around 3-4 children. While same-sex marriage is allowed, most people still opt for biological reproduction, although adoption is also an option.

In traditional family dynamics, fathers tend to take care of their sons more, while mothers often take care of their daughters. However, Startopia embraces diverse family structures, including patriarchal, matriarchal, or non-hierarchical setups.

Most families live together under one roof, but some individuals may choose to move to other villages to find a new family if desired. Teleportation technology is available, enabling quick and affordable visits between families.

Community and Proximity

Residents of Startopia typically live in close proximity to their village or community, fostering a sense of togetherness and connection. The option to relocate to another village is available for those seeking a new family or community.
