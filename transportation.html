Transportation in Startopia

Village Travel

In Startopia, villages are primarily traversed by walking, promoting a pedestrian-friendly environment where residents can easily explore their immediate surroundings.

Travel Beyond Villages

If individuals wish to venture outside their village, they have several transportation options available:

  • Biking: Residents can use bicycles for short-distance travel.
  • Driving: Private vehicles can be used for longer trips, although the road infrastructure is minimized due to reduced travel needs.
  • Train: A train network is available for convenient and sustainable long-distance travel.
  • Flying: Planes powered by stored solar energy provide efficient air travel.

Sustainable Energy and Teleportation

In line with Startopia's commitment to sustainability, all forms of transportation operate on stored energy from the Sun, harnessed through the Dyson Sphere. Gas-powered vehicles are not utilized in Startopia. Moreover, teleportation is a viable option, thanks to the advanced capabilities of harvesting and utilizing sun energy.
