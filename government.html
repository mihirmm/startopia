Startopia's Government

Citizens are known as Solians, and the government is headed by AI, which has been trained and evolved based on human behavior to act in the best interest of humans. They are incorruptible and unhackable due to their perfect programming.

Provinces are connected under Star Corp, an empire organization with a robot Congress. Each robot represents a single province, and political issues are resolved through popular vote. The World Congress, headed by Star Corp board members, oversees global affairs.

The military, consisting of both humans and robots, establishes energy harvesting on other planets to bring additional sun energy to Earth. A border military is established to protect the utopia from potential attacks.

There are no taxes in Startopia. Half of the world voluntarily joins the World Congress, while other countries can join if they wish. Each country's autonomy is transformed into a state within the World Congress.

There are 93 provinces for each country in the World Congress, ensuring representation and effective governance.

Laws

  • No murder.
  • No stealing.
  • No discrimination based on differences.
  • No drugs unless prescribed/recommended by a doctor.
  • No perjury.
  • Use a seatbelt when in a car.
  • Voting is mandatory.
  • Unhealthy food with levels of ingredients such as sugar and saturated fat higher than 5g is banned.
  • If one fails to follow the laws three times, they will be exiled from the utopia.
  • Follow the Star Corp legislature in the World Congress.

Guidances

  • Working is not necessary (AI will do most of the work).
  • English is the official language.
  • Socialize every day.
  • Eat a balanced diet of roughly 40% of total calories from carbohydrates, 30% from proteins, and 30% from fat. Consume at least 14,000 calories per week and no more than 28,000 calories per week.
  • Treat others, including AIs, with respect.
  • Refrain from lying.

The Bill of Rights

  • The right to healthy food and clean water.
  • The right to free education.
  • The right to information and transparency (regarding rationale behind AI decisions).
  • The right to equality.
  • The right to privacy.
  • The right to freedom of movement and to seek asylum from persecution.
  • The right to marry and to found a family.
  • The right to be free from torture and other cruel punishment.
  • The right to free speech.
