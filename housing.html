Startopia's Housing

Villages

Startopia's residents live in simple villages, forming tight-knit and close communities. Individuals have the freedom to choose which village they want to live in, and all villages are open for residents to join.

The Department of Housing of the World Congress (DHWC), led by robots elected by each country, determines the allocation and management of housing. They consider requests from residents who wish to change houses and ensure fair distribution.

You Choose!

Villages across the utopia exhibit different architectural styles, offering a diverse range of housing options. Some villages feature cottage houses, while others embrace colonial-style architecture.

Residents have the ability to choose the size of their house, within certain limits set by the DHWC. This ensures that everyone has access to adequate living space while maintaining a sense of equity within the community.
