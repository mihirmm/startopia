Technology in Startopia

Environmental Harmony and Energy

In Startopia, the AI has built a Dyson Sphere around the sun to harness its energy. To ensure ecosystem balance, plants have been genetically modified with GMOs to have maximum output per square foot. This fine-tuning of heat, light, and seasons has resulted in faster growth, bigger size, and enhanced resilience of plants.

The utilization of sun energy extends beyond our utopia and is facilitated by Star Corp's advancements:

  • Sun energy is used for the production of almost all materials.
  • Plants can be connected to machines and supercharged with raw energy, enabling them to grow about 9 times faster, ensuring efficient food and material production.
  • Mycelium, a sustainable alternative to plastics, is utilized, mitigating pollution concerns.

Technology and Healthcare

In Startopia, traditional phones are replaced by Heads Up Display (HUD) overlays integrated into contact lenses. These lenses provide a range of utilities such as GPS navigation, calculators, and AI assistants, enhancing convenience for individuals.

The AI is trained to assist in governing functions within villages, including maintenance, civil issues, recreation management, and community service.

Advanced healthcare technology has also been implemented:

  • Self-reproducing nanotechnology is injected into the bloodstream to monitor and maintain optimal levels of substances such as glucose, cholesterol, electrolytes, and more.
  • The nanotech aids in preventing blood clots, accelerates wound healing, and efficiently detects and destroys cancer cells, ensuring a cancer-free community.
  • Carbon emissions are effectively managed by AI-controlled machines that measure and regulate air quality, maintaining ideal gas levels in order to mitigate environmental impact.
