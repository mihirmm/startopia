Startopia's Economy

The Sun Dollar Currency

The Sun Dollar is the currency in our utopia, connected to Sun Energy, which powers various aspects of our community.

Sun Dollars and Energy Conversion

Sun Dollars are transferable into Sun Energy through a machine. Each village receives a monthly stipend of Sun Dollars based on its population, which can be distributed accordingly.

Usage and Limitations

Sun Dollars are necessary to fund imports and exports, support the military, and acquire materials for the community's enjoyment. However, basic necessities such as food, water, and shelter are already provided for, and do not require Sun Dollars for payment.

Sun Dollar Denominations

  • 5 kSoleil
  • 10 kSoleil
  • 50 kSoleil

(kSoleil is Startopia's unit of measurement for energy, and each coin represents a different amount of energy.)
