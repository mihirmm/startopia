Employment in Startopia

Residents of Startopia have the choice to work or not, with most jobs being assisted by AI technology. However, one notable exception is therapy, which is conducted solely by humans.

Popular Jobs

  • Teacher: The number of teachers per village varies based on population, with an optimal ratio of one teacher for every six students.
  • Auditor: Auditors ensure the smooth functioning of the community. They report problems to the AI for resolution and resource allocation to benefit the village.
  • Therapist: Individuals seek therapy from therapists who provide support and guidance, fostering happiness and emotional well-being within the community. They also pass down wisdom to younger generations.
  • Electrical Engineer: Electrical engineers troubleshoot and fix issues related to AI systems, ensuring their proper functioning. World Congress Engineers receive specialized training to handle AI senators.
  • Doctor: While AI aids in diagnosis, doctors provide second opinions and perform/interpret medical procedures such as surgery, intubation, and diagnostic tests.
